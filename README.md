# Project Title :
Amazon SaaS Sales Analytics Dashboard(Power BI).

## Project Description
This project analyzes Amazon SaaS sales data to understand business performance, regional trends, customer segmentation, and product profitability using Power BI.

---

## Objectives:
- Analyze overall SaaS business performance
- Identify top-performing regions and segments
- Understand customer and industry distribution
- Evaluate product and license profitability
  
---

## Tools Used
-Power BI
-Microsoft Excel

---

## Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Overview
2. Sales & Regional Performance
3. Customer & Market Segmentation Insights
4. Product Performance & Profitability Analysis

---

## Key Insights
- Enterprise segment contributes highest revenue
- Certain regions show high sales but low profit due to discounts
- Specific products generate high revenue with lower margins
